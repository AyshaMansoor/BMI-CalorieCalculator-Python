Diet suggestions: 

* Eat more frequently.

* Eat five to six smaller meals during the day rather than two or three large meals.

* Choose nutrient-rich foods like whole-grain breads, fruits and vegetables; dairy products; lean protein sources; and nuts and seeds.

* Food that is rich in good fats (like chestnuts, peanuts, avocado, coconut, olive oil and seeds) are great ways to increase calories in the diet.

* Try smoothies and shakes.

Exercise suggestions:

* Do strength training, it can help you gain weight by building up your muscles.

* Exercising regularly may also stimulate your appetite.
