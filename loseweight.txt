Diet suggestions:

* Eat smaller sized meals at regular times during the day, this helps burn calories at a faster rate.

* Eat more fruits and veggies, they are low in calories and fat, and high in fibre.They also contain plenty of vitamins and minerals.

* Drink plenty of water.

* Cut down on junk food

Exercise suggestions:

* Being active is key to losing weight and keeping it off.Find an activity you enjoy and are able to fit into your routine. Some exercises are:

Walking
Running
Cycling
Swimming
